About Us

Discover the science of natural beauty

Ayna – The Skin, Hair and Laser Clinic is a modern dermatology and aesthetic care center dedicated to enhancing natural beauty through safe, effective, and personalized treatments. Located in Rangeli Road, Biratnagar, our clinic combines advanced medical technology with a patient-centered approach to deliver high-quality skin, hair, and laser solutions.

At Ayna, we believe that healthy skin and hair are essential not only for appearance but also for confidence and overall well-being. Our goal is to provide treatments that are scientifically proven, minimally invasive, and tailored to the unique needs of each individual.

Led by Dr. Bibisha Baaniya, a qualified and experienced dermatologist, the clinic offers expert diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of skin and hair conditions. Dr. Baaniya is committed to staying updated with the latest advancements in dermatology and aesthetic medicine, ensuring that patients receive modern, safe, and effective care.

Our services include advanced skin treatments, hair restoration therapies, laser procedures, acne and scar management, and anti-aging solutions. We use high-quality equipment and follow strict hygiene and safety protocols to ensure the best outcomes for our patients.

What sets Ayna apart is our personalized approach. We take the time to understand each patient’s concerns, educate them about their condition, and design customized treatment plans that deliver natural-looking results.

Whether you are seeking medical dermatology care or aesthetic enhancement, Ayna Clinic is committed to helping you look and feel your best with confidence.
